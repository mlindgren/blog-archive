GPS Data: Kicking Horse Mountain Resort, 16 January 2011

Jan 31st, 2011 8:09 pm

Download this data in compressed KML format (viewable in Google Earth).

Statistics

  • Time: 3 hours, 2 minutes, 6 seconds*
  • Distance travelled: 29.5km (includes chair lifts)
  • Elevation change: 4,683m (in each direction)
  • Min/max elevation: 1,260/2,424m
  • Max/min slope: +54.9%/-67.4%
  • Max speed: 67.4km/h

*This was a short day because my party and I had to drive back to Calgary through Fernie; all of the other highways were closed.
