GPS Data: Sunshine Village, 18 December 2010

Dec 18th, 2010 9:29 pm

Download this data in compressed KML format (viewable in Google Earth).

Note: I didn’t replace the batteries in my GPS prior to recording data for this day, so it ran out of battery power around 2pm. The last several runs (probably 5-6 runs in total) are missing from the data.

Statistics

  • Time: 4 hours, 33 minutes, 37 seconds
  • Distance travelled: 49.9km* (includes chair lifts)
  • Elevation change: 5,092m (in each direction)
  • Min/max elevation: 2,040/2,679m
  • Max/min slope: +50.6%/-46.2%
  • Max speed: 72.8km/h*

* The GPS trip computer disagrees slightly with Google Maps for these items. I am using data from the trip computer as I assume it is more accurate.
